Tesla offers a range of electric vehicles that are designed to be both high-performance and environmentally friendly. Their models include the Model S, Model X, Model 3, Model Y, and Cybertruck.

The Model S is a luxury sedan that can travel up to 402 miles on a single charge and can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just 1.99 seconds. The Model X is a luxury SUV that can travel up to 371 miles on a single charge and can seat up to seven passengers. The Model 3 is a more affordable option that can travel up to 353 miles on a single charge and has a sleek and minimalist design. The Model Y is a compact SUV that can travel up to 326 miles on a single charge and offers a spacious interior.
